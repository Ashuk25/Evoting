Election Training

A fully customizable online training solution that ensures election staff are efficiently trained for the delivery of flawless election.


E-Training provides election staff with the detailed knowledge they require before and on election day via engaging, comprehensive and effective interactive learning available from any internet-connected device. With election staff geographically dispersed, electoral authorities face a major challenge when planning and undertaking their training. This typically includes managing short schedules, providing personnel with training material, and high costs associated with printing and delivery of material.Election Training helps ease these burdens by delivering fully customized, role-based online election training that meets the unique training needs that each election requires.


Assessment, training evaluation, and strategic analysis to determine election requirements and objectives, and the creation of comprehensive role-based custom courses.

Online training published and assigned for pre-election training and election day refresh. Administrative support, real-time training completion and reporting.

Thorough training cycle evaluation to identify areas of improvement for training content, channels and delivery.
